Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working together

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A brand development project typically takes 4-6 weeks, while comprehensive marketing campaigns can range from 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! I work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. I offer flexible service packages that can be tailored to fit different budgets and needs.

What industries do you specialize in?

I have experience across various industries including technology, healthcare, e-commerce, fashion, restaurants, and professional services. My strategic approach adapts to any industry's unique challenges and opportunities.

How do you measure success?

Success metrics are defined at the project's start based on your specific goals. These might include increased brand awareness, lead generation, sales growth, engagement rates, or ROI. I provide regular reports tracking these KPIs.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! I offer various ongoing support options including monthly retainers, quarterly strategy reviews, and ad-hoc consulting. Many clients prefer ongoing partnerships to continuously optimize their marketing efforts.

What's included in the initial consultation?

The initial consultation includes a comprehensive review of your current marketing efforts, discussion of your goals and challenges, market analysis, and preliminary recommendations. This 60-minute session is complimentary for qualified prospects.
